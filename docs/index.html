Arcticus project

Summary

In the Arcticus project, students at Mälardalens University (MDU) have been working with the goal of creating a “time-predictable and reliable remote control for a set of autonomous vehicles that are collaboratively working”. The work on the Arcticus has been done together with robotics students where they designed an actual system and our team that are from the project for dependable systems focused more on creating a dependable design and the implementation of customers needs and processes to achieve them. The focus that was decided upon for our contribution was on the turn-around time delay of below 100ms, by creating a dependable system meaning it also will become reliable. In the final report of the Arcticus project the lessons learnt will be shown. Based on the lesson learnt major improvements will be shown together with some conclusions that can be made from the project.
